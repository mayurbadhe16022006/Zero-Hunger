My Favourite Subject: Mathematics (Mumbai University Sem I)

Core Topic: Real Number System

Unit I covers the fundamental properties of the **Real Number System** ($\mathbb{R}$), including order properties and the concept of **Absolute Value**. Key concepts include the **l.u.b. axiom** (least upper bound) and **g.l.b.** (greatest lower bound).


Unit II: Sequences of Real Numbers

We explore the definition and convergence of sequences. A crucial theorem is that **every convergent sequence is bounded**.


Unit III: Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs)

This unit deals with solving **Linear Differential Equations** and **Exact Differential Equations**, focusing on applications like Newton's Law of Cooling.
