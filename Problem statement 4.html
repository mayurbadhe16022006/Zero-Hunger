My Favourite Sport: Cricket

About Cricket

Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players.

Why I Like Cricket

I love cricket because it teaches patience, teamwork, and strategy. I enjoy watching and playing cricket with my friends during weekends.

Famous Players

Some of my favorite cricket players are Virat Kohli, Rohit Sharma, and M.S. Dhoni. Their dedication and skills inspire millions of fans around the world.
